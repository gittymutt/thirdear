Step Four: Create the Future
Many of us would love to win the lottery, but it’s not a great selection here because it leaves you reliant on odds that are heavily stacked against you (approx. 1 in 176,000,000). As you might know, you have a greater chance of becoming President of the United States, becoming a movie star, being struck by lightning, or being crushed to death by a vending machine. Ruling the US or starring in a revenue-generating film comes with some good financial benefits and “perks,” but if neither of those are appealing, I recommend:
  1. Save the money you had been spending on lottery tickets, and start building your emergency fund.
  2. Go back one screen in this Wizard and select another resolution.
  3. If you really enjoy playing the lottery so much that you would do it even without the possibility of a jackpot, get a friend to agree to exchange tickets with you on birthdays, holidays and other special occasions.
