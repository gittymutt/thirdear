Money Conflicts: Step 3 - Play with the possibilities.
Yes! You read that correctly. It says play. I want you to remember what life was like before you created your current value of money—when you still thought what you wanted would just appear if you asked for it. Allow yourself to play with even the craziest of solutions, including the many ones not listed below.

If I could have this conflict resolve in any way possible,...
(Click or tap a choice.)

  • I would win the lottery.
  • I would get a higher-paying job (e.g., new job, promotion).
  • My partner would contribute more financially.
  • My loved ones would appreciate less-expensive gifts, meals, etc.
  • Someone who owes me money would pay me what I’m owed.
  • I wouldn’t have (something I don't want).
